# Session Terms

## Payment

Sessions are prepaid or run through a platform that protects payment.

Accepted structures:
- prepaid session,
- platform hourly contract,
- escrow milestone,
- paid invoice,
- crypto transfer confirmed before session.

Not accepted:
- pay after grade,
- pay after job approval without escrow,
- unpaid trial deliverables.

## Delivery

Each session includes:
- live diagnosis,
- guided explanation,
- practical next steps,
- short recap when useful.

## Academic / Platform Risk Control

The service is tutoring, coaching, debugging, and review.

I do not:
- take exams,
- impersonate a student or worker,
- bypass school or platform rules,
- provide plagiarism-only final submissions.

This protects both sides from bans, disputes, chargebacks, and unusable work.
